ReachOut

ReachOut is trying to solve on creating some possible ways in order to help the growing number of cancer cases inÂ Africa.

Cancer is emerging as a major public health problem in Sub-Saharan Africa (SSA) because of population aging and growth, as well as increased prevalence of key risk factors, including those associated with social and economic transition.

In females, the numbers of cases and rates of breast and cervical cancer are almost equal and comprise 50% of the overall cancer burden in SSA. In males, cancer of the prostate dominates in terms of the number of cases (51,900 cases, 27.9% of the total estimated cases in the region), followed by liver cancer (10.6% of the total) and Kaposi sarcoma (6.6% of the total).

Excerpt from Cancer.org

Our solution is to design a web platform which will allow cancer patients or relatives from Africa to connect with cancer specialist from UK or India. A patient should be able to request a second opinion by simply filling up a request form and wait for ReachOut approval if they met the documents required.

User Flow

Having clear idea how user would navigate the system helps to identify the essential pages and figure which part of the design might be difficult to access and figure out ways on simplifying how to access it.

Grid

Using a 12 columns grids a great way to make sure that information are displays properly on different displays. This is also flexible when working with modular components.

Modular Wireframes

Modular wireframes is a great way to explore more than one possible design solution. Using modular makes it easy to combine different layouts and see which one would work well together.

Mood Board

The next task is to explore visual direction and mood board is a good start and tool in order to initiate and give quick and easy to revise a look and feel. The keywords we used Experts, Provider, Quality, Positivity, Trust

Style Guide

After the client picked which one they like it is time to create a UI Style guide with elements to communicate the message of the design
